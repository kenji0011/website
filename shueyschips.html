Shuey's Kamote Chips

My 1st project in my college years. I used C# and Windows Forms to create a simple inventory management system for a small business. The system allows the user to add, edit, delete, and search for products in the inventory. Our client is Ms. Shuey, she have a small business that sells kamote chips and banana chips. Thanks to Mr. Ken Borce and Mr. Avril Carlos Lirio for helping me to finish this project.
